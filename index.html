bio

Electronic music blending nostalgia with urban groove, shaped by the rhythmic language of Ipanema and Lisbon’s diverse sonic textures. Focused on connection between people, coneqt uses music as a shared language. The result is both physical and emotional — built for movement, memory, and late-night moments.
